## Who is the customer?
Our primary customer is a new-to-insurance individual looking to obtain a personal insurance quote with minimal inputs, no account setup, and preferably via a mobile device. They want the ability to quickly decide if Evergreen is within their budget, provided a generally accurate monthly quote number. If they feel the process is too cumbersome, they will abandon it.

## What pain does Evergreen Quote remove?
The customer does not want to spend tons of time entering multiple data points to get an accurate insurance quote. After going through the effort, the price may not even be within budget, thus wasting time and turning the customer off to the Evergreen brand as a whole. This quick quoting tool allows them to get a fast response that is accurate enough to make a decision from.

## What does "good" look like at end of the week?
- The page should be styled with the Evergreen color-scheme, in easily readable font types/sizes, with intuitive selection options, and a responsive design intended for mobile users
- The quote functionality should work properly, calculating an estimated quote based on the user's coverage type and coverage amount inputs
- The page should include additional features for a rounded out look, to include testimonials, branding, and a menu (that will be implemented in the next iteration)

## What are we explicitly NOT doing this week?
- We are not using any official rating tables for the quote functionality
- We are not adding additional pages for the menu options to navigate to separate pages
- There will be no option for the user to create an account, save the quote, or proceed to purchasing steps 

## How will we know if it worked?
- The page will load in under a second and be interactive/responsive
- The quote will be returned in under a second, based off user input and rate lookup logic
