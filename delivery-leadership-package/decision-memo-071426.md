# Decision Memo — Scope Changes to Testimonials Section

**Date:** 7/14/26
**Author:** Tina
**Decision area:** Scope Changes to Testimonials Section

## Context
Project Sponsor provided a priority shift update this afternoon. We are no longer able to include the Testimonials section in this week's release due to missing approvals.

## Options considered

1. **Option A — Remove Testimonials Section.** Delete HTML section that would display the Testimonials on the app. Pro: We avoid lawsuits from the customers that may not have approved their comments being posted. Con: We lose that section of the app that could've encouraged more customers with positive experiences.
2. **Option B — Comment Out Testimonials Section.** Comment out HTML section that would display the Testimonials on the app, for now. Pro: This removes the section temporarily while we get approvals, but allows us to easily add it back in once those approvals are obtained. Con: We temporarily lose that section of the app that could've encouraged more customers with positive experiences.
3. **Option C — Leave In Testimonials Section.** Leave Testimonials visible in the app, despite the sponsor's warning. Pro: We get to provide positive experiences of others to new potential customers. Con: We leave ourselves open to lawsuits and compliance concerns from existing customers, possibly losing their business.

## Recommendation

Option B would be ideal, as it involves the least amount of coding changes, but also sets us up to reintroduce this feature to the app quickly in the future once approvals are obtained.
Option A would not be recommended, as it deletes coding already completed and introduces unnecessary rework.
Option C is too risky to implement as-is.

## Why

Option B provides flexibility in schedule while not losing work already done

## What would change my mind

If another change to scope or priority around the Testimonials section were to occur this week, I would need to revisit this decision.
